Our Story

Woven With Trust,
Rooted in Kerala

Nestled in the heart of Punalur, Radhas Textiles has been the go-to destination for families seeking quality textiles at honest prices. Our store carries the warmth of Kerala's rich textile heritage — from the pristine elegance of Kasavu sarees to vibrant everyday kurtis and fine dress materials.

We believe that every family deserves access to beautiful clothing without stretching their budget. That's why we've built a reputation for offering more variety and better prices than even the biggest online platforms — with the added warmth of personal, friendly service.
